<h3>ABOUT ME</h3>
<hr>
<i>Here's a little more about my personal life, if you're interested:</i>

I have always aspired to be an engineer. Tinkering with things and solving problems always came naturally and put my mind at ease. Up until college I
was never really sure what kind of engineer I wanted to be, only that I wanted to be one. I settled into electrical because I found myself drifting towards
wiring and programming in every school project on which I worked.

College has only reinforced my enjoyment of electrical engineering. I enjoy the variety of interconnected yet vastly different topics I get exposed to. I enjoy
working on interesting projects and pushing myself to be the best I can be. This website, for example, is one of the weird tangents I've gotten myself into.
Learning to play around with HTML and CSS isn't particularly helpful to a controls engineer - it can be pretty fun though.



In my free time I attempt to host a weekly session of Dungeons and Dragons, where I improvise and story-tell for hours on end. Additionally, I spend
quite a bit of time reading. I've got a soft spot for fantasy and sci-fi because I grew up on them. Roadside Picnic and Dune are the stories I find myself
thinking of most often, although I'm a sucker any good storytelling.

You can get into contact with me at desantis.max.a@gmail.com or max.desantis@okstate.edu
