<h3>ABOUT ME</h3>
<hr>

I grew up in central Oklahoma and have always aspired to being an engineer. I was never really sure what kind of engineer I wanted to be,
I just knew that I loved tinkering and solving problems. Sometime in high school I fell into the role of programmer and never went back.

In my free time I attempt to host a weekly session of Dungeons and Dragons, where I improvise and story-tell for hours on end. Additionally, I spend
quite a bit of time reading. I've a soft spot for fantasy and sci-fi because I grew up on them. In my mind, Roadside Picnic and Dune
compete for first place.
